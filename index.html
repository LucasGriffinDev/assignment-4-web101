Our Story

Located off the main street, Bruno's Pizzeria has been the heart and soul of the neighborhood. For years, the aroma of our freshly baked pizzas has wafted through the streets, bringing joy to every corner. While we're traditional at heart, we're embracing the future with our new online ordering system. We hope it brings even more convenience to your Bruno's Pizzeria experience.

Why Order Online?

Due to the increasing demand for takeaway, we've decided to go digital! By ordering online, you help us serve you faster, ensuring that every pizza is as fresh and delicious as you expect. Plus, no more waiting on the phone! Choose your favorite pizza, customize it if you like, and we'll get to baking.

Location & Hours

We're just off the main street, impossible to miss with the delicious scent of pizzas in the air. Join us, dine-in, or simply pick up your online order. We're open from 10 AM to 10 PM daily.
